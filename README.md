# SEO Automator ‚Äî SerpAPI + OpenAI

A Streamlit-based toolkit that combines **Google Search Console**, **Screaming Frog exports**, **SerpAPI**, and **OpenAI** to automate SEO workflows: rank tracking, content briefs, internal linking, competitive audits, and more.

---

## üöÄ Features

- **Internal Links**  
  Discover opportunities for natural internal linking with SerpAPI `site:` searches and AI-generated anchor text suggestions.

- **Rank Track**  
  Monitor keyword rankings using SerpAPI with caching, daily snapshots, and baseline comparisons.

- **Content Audit**  
  Compare your pages against competitors: headings, on-page signals, n-gram density, coverage overlap, and structured insights.

- **Content Brief**  
  Generate structured SEO briefs using SERP analysis, AI Overviews, and semantic keyword expansions.

- **Competitive Audit**  
  Analyze keyword gaps, competitor top pages, inlink anchors, and receive quick recommended actions.

- **Search Console Analyzer**  
  Upload GSC exports (Excel, CSV, or ZIP) or Screaming Frog GSC CSVs to explore queries, pages, devices, and more.

- **Screaming Frog Analyzer**  
  Process Screaming Frog exports for on-page checks, n-gram keyword density, and internal link mapping.
